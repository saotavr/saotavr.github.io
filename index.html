Our (Virtual) Reality

Using SAOTA's virtual reality (VR) system, one can be immersed in virtual space by viewing imagery on a head mounted display (HMD)—an HD screen attached to clever head-tracking technology or a mobile phone. Virtual reality gives our architects and clients the benefit of a digital representation of a building allowing for quicker collaborative design and iterative critiques. Clients can experience presence in their design at the correct scale and lighting within a resolved digital representation of their project. This allows for better a better understanding of the architecture prior to construction.

Mobile Application

SAOTA VR is an application that allows users to explore SAOTA's architectural designs in virtual reality (VR). The app is FREE and once downloaded can be viewed with or without a Google Cardboard viewer; a Samsung Gear VR version will be available in 2016. At present, SAOTA VR contains six projects with multiple viewpoints digitally rendered in 360 degrees, providing users a window into a world of SAOTA designs.
